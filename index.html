Welcome to F C N Arts & Sports Club

The youth as a whole is a great power. Proper utilization of this power for the development of the society is one the most important thing. Founded in 2019 by a group of young people of Neravth. Since its establishment it has been actively organizing and participating in various sports & socio-cultural activities in furtherance of its objectives of grooming the youth as honorable and accomplished citizens of the country.

Our Aim

To promote culture, sports, religious and other youth activities. To promote and activate the sense of social service among the residence of the FCN community. To promote friendship, fellowship and natural understanding among the memebers of youth club.

About

FCN Arts and Sports Club Affiliated by Nehru Yuva Kendra. FCN is a organisation of youth who are striving towards the achievement of common goals and objectives. It is formed, managed and run by youth themselves for the growth and development of youth in particular and the community in general.
